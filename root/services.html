Integrated Development & Spatial Planning


An integral part of urban and regional planning

Agizo Solutions seeks to contribute to the transformation of the space economy by providing integrated development planning solutions.

Integrated development and spatial planning services form an integral part of urban and regional planning. Its primary function is to necessitate equitable utilisation of scarce resources in society, particularly land. As a multi-pronged development approach, spatial planning engages with issues aligned to economic development, social development, land-use management, environmental planning and infrastructure development. As a rational decision-making process, spatial planning is dependent on the formulation of credible and implementable planning tools.

These tools include:

  •   Spatial development frameworks
  •   Integrated development plans
  •   Environmental planning frameworks
  •   Local economic development frameworks

Spatial planning for the public sector seeks to influence the future spatial distribution of activities and land use management. In a developing country like South Africa, spatial planning has a mammoth task of fostering a balance between demands for socio-spatial justice with the need to protect the environment and economic growth. Spatial planning is therefore an important lever for promoting sustainable development and improving the quality of life.

Agizo Solutions seeks to contribute to the transformation of the space economy by providing integrated development planning solutions. The company uses a systematic method of spatial planning in which geo-informatics and multi-criteria decision analysis tools are integrated to provide effective tools rational decision-making processes in the public and private sector.

Agizo uses cutting-edge scientific approaches to provide national, regional and municipal governments with the following development planning solutions:

  •   Integrated Development Plans (IDP) and Spatial Development Frameworks (SDF)
  •   Provincial Growth and Development Strategies (PGDSS) and Spatial Plans
  •   Implementation of spatial planning and land use management (SPLUMA) tools
  •   Identification of suitable land for specific uses
  •   Regional and municipal economic development plans
  •   Environmental planning instruments and impact assessments
  •   Formulation of integrated development planning policies and frameworks

Agizo Solutions also provides the private sector with spatial planning services such as Identification, selection and differentiation of markets, retail store catchments and the distribution of services and resources.

Integrating GIS and multi-criteria / objective decision analysis to address
complex challenges

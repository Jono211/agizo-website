WHO WE ARE

Agizo Solutions is a professional consultancy in the areas of strategic spatial planning, land-use planning and management, development planning, geographic information systems, land surveying and project management.

Our goal is to provide client-oriented services to develop professional, scientifically-informed solutions to the many complex challenges faced by strategic and operational decision-makers every day.
